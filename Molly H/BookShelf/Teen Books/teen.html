Land of Stories

By Chris Colfer

When Alex and Conner Bailey get a story book from their grandmother, they don't expect to go into the book and get trapped in a world filled with all the fairytale characters! They meet Cinderella, Snow White, Sleeping Beauty, the Evil Queen, and more, in their journey to escape the Land of Stories!

Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

The CandyMakers

By Wendy Mass

At the Life is Sweet candy factory, Logan, Daisy, Miles, and Philip are about to compete in a candymaking competition of a lifetime. Who will be able to make a candy more delicious than the Oozing Crunchorama or the Neon Yellow Lightning Chew? Throughout the book they realize that some things are a little fishy and they have to figure it out!

Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★

Harry Potter

By J.K. Rowling

Everybody should know the Harry Potter books so I am only going to do a short summary. Harry Potter is a wizard who has been living in a house with muggles who treat him very poorly. He finds out he is wizard and goes to a wizard and witch school that changed his life.

Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Percy Jackson the Lightning Thief

By Rick Riordan

Percy is a 12 year old boy living with his mom, he's just a normal kid, until strange stuff happens at school and he finds out that all greek mythology stories actually happened. In a journey to return Zeus's lightning bolt he finds out who his father is!

Rick Riordon also has many more books that are really good!

Rating: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★
