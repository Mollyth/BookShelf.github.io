Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry Potter's third year at Hogwarts starts off badly when he learns deranged killer Sirius Black has escaped from Azkaban prison and is set on murdering the teenage wizard. A swarm of nasty Dementors is sent to protect the school from Sirius Black. A mysterious new teacher helps Harry learn to defend himself, but what is his secret tie to Sirius Black?
