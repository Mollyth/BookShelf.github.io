Jeremy Fink and The Meaning of Life

Jeremy and Lizzy's summer takes a surprising turn when a mysterious wooden box arrives in the mail. Engraved on the top are the words "The Meaning of Life. For Jeremy Fink on his 13th birthday". Jeremy and Lizzy solve clues to open the box and discover the meaning of life.
