A Mango-Shaped Space

Thirteen-year-old Mia Winchell is far from ordinary: she suffers from a rare condition called synesthesia, the symptoms in which a person can see sounds, smell colors, or taste shapes. But because she has kept it a secret from everyone, she appears to be the most normal kid in her family.
