Land of Stories A Grimm Warning

Conner Bailey thinks his fairytale adventures are behind him-until he finds a mysterious clue left by the famous Brothers Grimm. When all signs point to disaster for the Land of Stories, Conner and his sister, Alex must join forces with their friends and enemies to save the day. But nothing can prepare them for the coming battle... or the secret that will change the twins' lives forever.
