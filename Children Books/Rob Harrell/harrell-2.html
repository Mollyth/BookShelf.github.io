Life of Zarf: The Troll Who Cried Wolf

Even after rescuing the king from deadly Snuffweasels, Zarf is scum on the bottom rung of the middle school social ladder. After all, he is still a troll. But when his best friend Kevin Littlepig is kidnapped as part of a evil plan to avenge the Big Bad Wolf... well, that’s a new low even for Zarf. A wolfish rebellion is begining, and Little Red Ridinghood’s daughter,who just so happens to be Zarf's crush, is their next target... unless Zarf can get that troll brain of his to come up with a solution.
