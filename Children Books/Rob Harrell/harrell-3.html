Life of Zarf: Troll Overboard

As a troll, Zarf is already at the bottom of the food chain when it comes to popularity at Cotswin Middle School. So when his Gramps shows up at career day, Zarf is sure his cool factor will hit an all-time low. What he doesn’t expect is for the super-obnoxious Prince Roquefort to take an interest in Gramps’s nautical know-how. Zarf is sure the prince is up to something... but why would he want Gramps to take him on a boat ride very close to the Berundi Rectangle? And why does he keep mumbling about magic beans?
