Last Kids On Earth and the Nightmare King

Life after the zombie apocalypse is pretty good for Jack Sullivan: he lives in a tree fort with his best friends and has a crew of monster buddies. But then Jack's friends make a strange discovery: they may not be the last kids on earth, after all. This is great news for everyone... except Jack. Once they've found other humans, his friends won't stick around for long! Jack's only hope for keeping things the way they are is to prove that everything here is perfect. One problem: it's hard convincing his friends that everything is great when they're being hunted by an evil Nightmare King who won't rest until Earth has been devoured. Maybe life after the apocalypse is more complicated than Jack thought.
